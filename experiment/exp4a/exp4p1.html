Analysis and Synthesis of Logic Functions using Dual 4:1 Multiplexer(IC 74153)

INSTRUCTION

  1. Apply \(V_{CC}\) and low level voltage to ground(GND)

  3. For all the combinations of the select inputs A,B verify that both the LEDs are ON or not.

  5. Note: Red button symbolize as Low (L), Green button symbolize as High(H).
